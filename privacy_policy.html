Privacy Statement

The statement highlights our privacy practices regarding Personal data that we hold about you.

Personal Information that you give to Anand

We take the upmost care to ensure that the personal information we obtain from you is used only for the purposes we describe in this Privacy Policy or at the time of collection. When you are asked to sign in or to register with us, you may be asked to provide your name, telephone number, email address, and/or delivery address. In the event you opt to provide us with personal information, we will only use it for the purpose specified.

How Anand uses your Personal information

We will always have a lawful basis when we collect and use your personal information, such as necessity to perform a contract that we have with you, because you consented to our use of your personal information. Specifically, we may use your personal information to:

  1. To Process and manage your orders.

  3. Create and deliver personalized promotions to grab our offers.
  4. Communicate with you about Anand, including communications about your reservations or food orders
  5. Communicate with you in connection with our marketing efforts to improve our service on a daily go.

Data Recipients, Disclosure of Personal data

Anand does not share your Personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without seeking your prior permission. Anand will seek your consent prior to use or share Personal data for any purpose beyond the requirement for which it was originally collected. When required, Anand may disclose Personal data to external law enforcement bodies or regulatory authorities, in order to comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and/or alteration of information under our control. The data resides behind a firewall, with access restricted to authorized Anand personnel.
